Glade's Plans for the Future

Glade is excited about the future. He has dreamed for a long time of playing in a large band (7-10 musicians). He has taken guitar lessons for many years to prepare for playing in a band eventually. He is learning how to code so he can get into a career that will let him afford to buy a home and so many guitars he doesn't have the time to play them all. He also wants to eventually create a website with instrustional videos and guitar lessons. He has learned music from many sources, but he thinks he has a unique view on learning music and would like to share that with people.
